OJT Experience

Reflection

During my internship at Asurion Philippines, I spent almost 500 hours learning and gaining valuable experience. I not only acquired technical skills but also developed the right work attitude for a company. Asurion Philippines was my first-ever company, marking the beginning of my career and work journey.

Initially, I felt nervous and unsure, not knowing what to expect in a company. However, with the support of my team, I overcame those fears and ended up enjoying my time there. I was introduced to new applications like PowerApps, Power Automate, PowerBI, and PowerShell for development purposes.

These tools played a crucial role in the projects I worked on at Asurion Philippines. I utilized PowerApps and Power Automate to create two mobile applications. The first one allows users to activate a maintenance window from DynaTrace with a simple button toggle. This eliminates the manual process of going to DynaTrace and activating a maintenance window, making it more convenient by enabling users to do it from their phones.

The second mobile application helps the team manage their overtime and offset hours. Team members input their overtime and offset hours, and the app sends an approval request to managers. Managers can then easily approve or reject requests using the mobile app, streamlining the process for everyone involved. This alleviates the manual encoding and use of excel when doing the activity.

Using PowerShell and PowerBI, I extracted utilization data from the hosts our team monitors on DynaTrace. I achieved this by calling the DynaTrace API in PowerShell and exporting the data to Excel. This Excel file served as the data source for the PowerBI dashboard we developed. The dashboard was instrumental in presenting crucial information to team members, highlighting servers that required attention and action. It provided valuable insights that helped our team make informed decisions about server management.

Overall, my internship at Asurion Philippines was incredibly valuable for my career. It set a standard for the kind of company I aim to join in the future. Working with genuinely good and kind people made the experience memorable. The supervisors ensured I gained ample learning experiences and a solid understanding of DevOps. I will cherish my time at Asurion and carry the lessons with me as I move forward in my career.
